Our Story

Where Fitness Meets Art

Founded by Ajith Kumar in 2015, BRAVE X FIT & RDC Dance Studio brings together the worlds of fitness, dance, and martial arts under one roof. Our mission is to create a holistic training environment that nurtures both physical and mental strength.

Proudly based in Mandya, Karnataka, we've trained thousands of students from all walks of life - from fitness enthusiasts to professional dancers and MMA fighters. Our unique approach blends traditional training methods with modern techniques.
